About me

Who am I?

My name's Tom Duncan, I'm a freelance web developer based in Coventry and Birmingham in the West Midlands. I plan, design, build and maintain a variety of websites and web apps. At the moment my work is primarily focused on advanced Wordpress themes with modern, responsive frontend development.

I love crafting rich, attractive user experiences that are clear and easy to use.

My aim is always to solve complex problems with simple solutions.

Background & experience

My background is in Computer Science – I have a degree from the University of Nottingham – which gives me a deep understanding of the underlying concepts and processes and involved in all aspects of development. I am well equipped to learn new skills and adapt my knowledge and logical ability to many different programming languages and frameworks.

Equally as valuable is my three years of experience working in the industry. From 2014 to 2017 I worked for a digital agency in Birmingham. I was fortunate enough to work on projects for many different clients, from local businesses to major brands and organisations, such as Mitie, the City Bridge Trust, Allen & Overy, Metfriendly, the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development, and lots more.
